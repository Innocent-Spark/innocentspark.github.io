# Introduction

We are Joseph and Dmitry, an Anglo-American team based between Austin and Berlin, with a shared vision for a more connected way of living. We've discovered a breathtaking 5.3-hectare paradise along the crystal-clear Rio Paiva in northern Portugal—a rare find in today's Europe that offers both pristine nature and the opportunity to build something extraordinary together.

Imagine waking up to birdsong, swimming in your private river beach, and harvesting olives from your own trees—all while being part of a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who value both independence and connection. This isn't just a property investment; it's an invitation to co-create a lifestyle that many dream of but few achieve.

Inspired by successful community projects like [Empire](https://tynan.com/empire/) and [Projekt Kamp](https://projectkamp.com/mission.html), we're transforming a historic hamlet into a thriving hub of creativity, sustainability, and meaningful human connection—all while preserving the natural beauty that makes this place so special.
